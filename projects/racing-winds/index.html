racing_winds –
Weather Data Visualizing Betting Game (2019)

// OpenWeatherMap API, Processing

racing_winds video

Racing Winds is a betting game centered around wind speeds. Players are prompted to type int the name of any city in the world that they believe is the windiest at that moment. The game then retrieves and visualizes the current wind speeds for the chosen locations. The player who guesses the windiest city wins.
