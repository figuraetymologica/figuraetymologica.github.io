Intimacy over Distance (2021)

// Arduino, drawing tablet, p5.js, Webserial, Websocket.io

pathfinder video

Pathfinder is one out of four artifacts created as part of the the Teleflirt project, which explored alternative methods of interpersonal communication at a distance. The design and construction of the artifact were done by Marina Schwarz. I handled the technological aspects (hardware, software).

The artifact enable two people to communicate through a shared pixel-based drawing and search game. First, Person A creates a drawing for Person B by coloring pixels on their device. Then, Person B searches for the drawing on their own device by moving a pen across the pixel grid. When the pen hovers over a colored pixel, it vibrates. Person B can then color in that pixel, gradually revealing the drawing, one step at a time.
